Favorite Book - Mindset by Professor Carol Dweck

Mindset is a book that takes a deep dive into two types of thinking, a growth mindset and a fixed mindset. Professor Dweck with psychology and proven experiments, explains the benefits and impact of a growth mindset, which is the belief that all skills can be learned and there is no belief of being a natural towards a skill, all people work hard to achieve accomplishment
